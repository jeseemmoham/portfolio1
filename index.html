Wheels to Watts

Wheels to Watts is a system that converts mechanical energy from bicycle motion into electrical energy. It uses a dynamo, voltage regulator, and battery storage to generate stable power for charging devices like mobile phones and powering LEDs.

Key Features

  • Dynamo-based energy harvesting from bicycle wheel rotation
  • Voltage regulator circuit for stable 5V output
  • Rechargeable battery storage system
  • USB charging port for mobile devices
  • LED lighting powered by pedal energy
  • Eco-friendly and sustainable power solution
